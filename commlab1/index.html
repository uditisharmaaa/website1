About me

Hi! My name is Uditi and I made this website for my Communications Lab class. I'm a sophomore majoring in CS and IM, with a minor in applied math. I'm from India and I grew up in Dubai. I'm passionate about trying food and cuisines from different countries and NYUAD is a great place to do this!
There's a variety of cusines such as Indian, Arab, Mediterranian, South American, Japanese (sushi), and of course, American. As a vegetarian, I initially struggled with finding healthy, low-oil, and high protein food options - but over my freshman year, I now have spots I can definitely count on for a good satiating meal. I show you some of my and my friends' favorite food spots, and invite you to send me your favorite foods!
